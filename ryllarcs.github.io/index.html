Hello, welcome to Ryllar Cleaning Services

As Ryllar Cleaning Services we provide affordable and customized cleaning solutions to our customers all around Johannesburg.

We presently service small offices and homes according to our customers' needs with just a few staff members by providing an array of quality services.

Services

Office cleaning

A well-cleaned office not only emphasizes the professionalism
of your company, but also creates a work atmosphere your
employees can enjoy to help them be at their best.

Home Cleaning

The busy lives we live do not permit us to clean the places we live in. We are here to take care of that while you concentrate on resting in a refreshing home after an exhausting day at the office.

Gardening 

Proper garden care can increase the quality and longevity of your plants, fruits, and veggies. A beautiful garden gives a refreshing welcome to you and your guests. Enjoy watching or basking in your well-taken care of garden. 

Fumigation and pest control

Why keep bugs and pests in your home when we can get rid of them and give you some peace of mind? Our Fumigation and Pest Control services are highly effective and long lasting way of exterminating and getting rid of rodents, mosquitos, bedbugs, termites and ants.

Rental property cleaning 

We clean properties in between moving out and moving in tenants. After, repainting and renovating, allow us to give your property a refreshing touch before the new tenant moves in.

Packing for moving house 

Moving from one house to another is a stressful experience, moreover packing of stuff in this busy city lifestyle! Why stress over what we can do? We will neatly pack and label your stuff making it easy to unpack at your new home.
